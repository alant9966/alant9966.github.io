About Us

Founded in 2018 under the name of Capriccio, Viking Vocalists is a music club that aims to promote the a capella genre of choral music through concert performances, community events, and other related activities. As Lynbrook's one and only a capella singing club, Viking Vocalists meets weekly to rehearse for performances, socialize with fellow members, and most importantly, to simply enjoy music with others of the same passion. Our opportunities for performance include Winter Concert, Pops Concert, and many others throughout the year.
